## Palette Drift Follow-up

After this album was added, the Shino gallery was checked for dark-color drift.
The text-level scan found 47 Shino images, with 39 mentioning black, navy,
charcoal, or graphite in their alt text, and 28 mentioning black. This is not a
pixel-level audit, but it confirms that Shino's outfit language has been
leaning too heavily on dark garments.

Future Shino albums should use `prompts/character-shino.md` and record place
and palette plans before generation. Black and navy should remain available
for lab, night, and DJ contexts, but Shino's identity should be carried by her
quietly confident gaze, hair, fish/lab/aquarium/music objects, city movement,
and behavior, not by repeating black/navy clothing as the default.

## Gaze And Place Follow-up

Shino's baseline gaze should not be described as sleepy. Morning slowness can
exist as a situational habit, but her normal eyes should read as quiet
self-possession: observant, steady, and carrying confidence under the surface.

Future albums should also use the main-story city more broadly. The lab,
aquarium, bar, and apartment are important, but Shino should keep moving
through harbor markets, old town lanes, tram and station detours, libraries or
bookshops, hills and old walls, seaside cafes, pools, neighborhood lobbies,
and the standards-lab district as the story calls for them.
