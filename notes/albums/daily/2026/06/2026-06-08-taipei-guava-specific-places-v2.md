## Notes

This remake keeps the same date and lucky color but answers the place question
more directly. The previous Taipei set had a riverside and balcony that were
too generic. This version names four usable Taipei scenes and records that
specificity in the album, index, and generation log.

The first image was regenerated because the initial market result made the
18-19-adult chest opening stronger than planned. The accepted result still has
a warm-weather tank and open shirt instead of forced high-neck coverage,
because the stated goal is natural clothing for humid Taipei rather than
defensive overcoverage.

The fourth image originally failed when the prompt combined Red House, night,
club motion, and multiple constraints. It succeeded after shortening the prompt
and describing Ximending as a pedestrian alley with no readable signs.
